Rural Health Transformation Program (RHTP)
========================================== Main_Content Background
​​[​​​H.R. 1](https://www.congress.gov/crs-product/IN12578),
enacted into law on July 4,
2025, created a $50 billion fund called the [Rural Health Transformation
Program](https://www.cms.gov/priorities/rural-health-transformation-rht-program/rural-health-transformation-rht-program)[(RHTP)](https://www.cms.gov/priorities/rural-health-transformation-rht-program/rural-health-transformation-rht-program).
Maryland's Award On December 29, 2025, Maryland was pleased to
receive a notice of award from the [Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services
(CMS)](https://www.cms.gov/newsroom/press-releases/cms-announces-50-billion-awards-strengthen-rural-health-all-50-states)
for $168,180,837.61 for budget period one, of five, of the Rural
Health Transformation Program. Maryland is currently in discussion with
CMS regarding approved programs and adjusted budget details.​See additional
information[here](https://health.maryland.gov/newsroom/Pages/Moore-Miller%20Administration%20secures%20$168%20million%20in%20federal%20funding%20to%20advance%20health%20care%20in%20rural%20Maryland%20communities.aspx).
Updates will be posted on this page as available, including
opportunities to apply for the RHTP Committee and to submit proposals
for competitive subrecipient funding. Please continue to follow for
more information. Rural Health Transformation Committee: Accepting
Applications! The Maryland Department of Health is committed
to gathering robust input from rural communities to guide RHTP
activities. We invite rural residents and organizations to join the RHT
Committee, to offer feedback and input to help guide implementation
of Maryland’s award. The State seeks volunteers representing the
perspectives of rural Maryland-based organizations and/or communities
to participate.​[See additional information and application instructions
here!​](https://health.maryland.gov/pophealth/Pages/Rural-Health-Transformation-Program-Committee.aspx)
R​​​HTP Application and Timeline CMS posted a Notice of Funding
Opportunity for states to apply for the RHTP on September 15, 2025. To
gather feedback to inform the proposal, the Department: * Released a
Request for Information (RFI) invitation on September 9, 2025. The RFI
closed on September 30 and the Department received over 325 responses. *
Held an introductory public webinar on September 15, 2025. Slides are
available[here](https://health.maryland.gov/pophealth/Documents/Rural%20Health/RFI%20RHTP%20Slides%20-%20Public%20Webinar.pdf).
* Collaborated ​with local health departments to organize 17 [local listening
sessions](https://health.maryland.gov/pophealth/Pages/Rural%20Health%20Transformation%20Program%20Listening%20Sessions.aspx)
held in-person and virtually throughout rural Maryland. More
than 250 people participated. * On October 20, 2025, the
Department presented a summary of the feedback and an outline
of our draft Rural Health Transformation Plan. [Slides are available
here.](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1pxlFQvnlcYF1q0XyhDW1oHBf8HNILmYJ/view?usp=sharing)
* Maryland submitted an application by the
deadline posted. Maryland’s submitted progam narrative is [available
here](https://health.maryland.gov/pophealth/Documents/Rural%20Health/RHTP/MD-Project-Narrative-CMS-RHT-26-001-Cover-Updated-Watermark-%283%29.pdf).
​ A summary of Maryland’s [proposal is available
here](https://health.maryland.gov/pophealth/Documents/Rural%20Health/RHTP/FINAL%20One%20Pager.pdf)​
and below. ​![Image
