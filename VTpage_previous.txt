H.R.1/Rural Health Transformation Fund ======================================
### What’s the Rural Health Transformation Fund? There is a major new
funding opportunity on the horizon called the [Rural Health Transformation Fund
(RHTF)](https://www.cms.gov/priorities/rural-health-transformation-rht-program/rural-health-transformation-rht-program),
created by [H.R. 1](https://www.congress.gov/bill/119th-congress/house-bill/1).
This program sets aside $50 billion over five years—starting in
2026. The goal of the fund is to promote bold, innovative ideas that
make care more accessible, higher quality, and better tailored to
the needs of rural communities. The [Notice of Funding Opportunity
(NOFO)](https://www.grants.gov/search-results-detail/360442) for the RHTP
was posted on September 15th. The application closes in early November,
with awards expected by . [Rural Health Transformation (RHT) Program |
CMS](https://www.cms.gov/priorities/rural-health-transformation-rht-program/rural-health-transformation-rht-program)
